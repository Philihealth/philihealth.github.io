A Year of Daily Weight Loss Motivational Quotes for Weight Watchers

No matter where you’re at on your weight loss journey, these motivational quotes for weight loss will help you to get moving. Whether it’s a little or a lot, losing weight and keeping it off is no easy task.

Why are motivational weight loss quotes so important to our success?

Our thoughts are powerful. What we tell ourselves matters.

When we’re happier with a positive mindset , we tend to make healthier decisions , including those that lead to weight loss —such as saying no to junk food, getting enough sleep, moving more, taking care of your body and eating healthy.

Related: Get Happy Lose Weight; The Mind-Body Link That Matters

Unfortunately that voice in our head that says “give up” is usually louder than the one that says “keep going.” So the next time you’re feeling discouraged, read one of these encouraging, inspirational, motivational quotes to yourself to lift your mood.

How can I get myself motivated to lose weight?

Losing weight and keeping it off isn’t easy. But neither is being overweight and out of shape.

As far as I’m concerned working to be in good health, in good spirits and able to fit into my clothes is WORTH the effort. After years of struggle I feel like I’ve finally unlocked the secret to lasting weight loss success:

Find your why. Have a strong reason for wanting to lose weight. For me it’s all about being healthy, happy, and wanting to grow old gracefully and vibrantly. I want to be able to feel good so I can continue to travel and pursue my dreams.

Do it your way. It’s important to lose weight your way , not someone else’s. You need to find changes that you are willing to make forever, not jus for a day or a week or until you’ve reached your goal.

Focus on teeny tiny changes. It’s much better to commit to a little change that you can actually accomplish regularly than a huge one that you are never able to get done. Years ago one of my favorite yogas told me to “only do as much yoga as feels good” because “a little yoga done often is better than a lot of yoga done rarely.” Turns out this is the secret to any lasting behavior change.

Celebrate you successes . We are hardwired to do what makes us feel good. Find healthy ways to celebrate when you accomplish your new habits, hit your targets and achieve your goals, regardless of how small they seem. This positive feeling can help propel you forward.

What have good health quotes got to do with it?

Reading and collecting motivational and inspirational quotes including ones for good health is one of my favorite strategies for filling my mind with the positive thinking that can lead to increased health and happiness. I’ve gathered many of my favorites here in hopes they benefit you too.
